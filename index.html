Booking

The online booking system allows you to check availability, make your reservation and register yourself to the waiting list for the date of your choice if there is no availability. Please note that the waiting list is under no circumstances a confirmation or valid reservation but only a first step in registering to our system. We will contact you only in the event of availability due to a cancellation. In order to guarantee your reservation, we will require the references of a credit or debit card.

For any further information or modification for a confirmed reservation, you can contact us:
- by phone at +91 8149 21 2466,
- by email: reservation@delizioso.in


Booking and Cancellation Policy.

For any reservation we will require the references of a credit card as a guarantee. The card is secured by the system and we have no access to your data. Reservation will be considered as confirmed once the credit card details will be provided. Your reservation can be cancelled or modified, free of charge until 3 days prior to your scheduled meal. Below that, in the event of non-attendance or late cancellation, we will charge the amount of 150 rupees per person. We hope that we have met your expectations and remain at your disposal for any additional information you may require.

We look forward to welcoming you to Delizioso in the near future.
