ABOUT US

Welcome to Delizioso, our home located in Mumbai city, where we invite guests to celebrate life and share sundry blends of modern and traditional Lebanese cuisine. Our goal at Delizioso is to unify our guest’s cultures and spirits with our own, expressing passion and generosity in the cuisine we share. Executive Chef and Owner karan kalani excellently crafts a melting pot of Lebanese, Indian, Spanish, Mediterranean inspired cuisine mixed with Mumbai attitude. Flavorful herbs and spices combine textures to appease broad-based palates. Our attentive staff ensures that your entire dining experience at Delizioso is stellar from beginning to end. Each passing of our shared plates and the breaking of fresh Mumbai's pita opens up a new conversation. Whether part of a party of fifty or a party of two, your experience throughout our elegant, yet comfortable space is eventful. We are sharing our long held Lebanese-Mediterranean and Inidan traditions with a modern contemporary twist. You will feel both close to home and apart of something new.

OUR PEOPLE

Delizioso is a place for family, the one you’re born into and the one you create around food, love and friendship. Our attentive staff ensures that your entire dining experience with us is stellar from beginning to end.
Sanjeev Kapoor (born 10 April 1964) is an Indian celebrity chef, entrepreneur and television personality. Kapoor stars in the TV show Khana Khazana, which is the longest running show of its kind in Asia. He also launched his own Food Food channel, in January 2011.After working in many hotels, he became the Executive Chef of the Centaur Hotel in Mumbai. He is also the recipient of the Best Executive Chef of India Award by H & FS and the Mercury Gold Award at Geneva, Switzerland by Indian Federation of Culinary Associations. Singapore Airlines recruited him as one of the members of its International Culinary Panel. He is also the brand ambassador for Sweekar Advanced, a sunflower oil brand in India.
Massimo Bottura (conceived 30 September 1962) is an Italian restaurateur and a famous Italian Chef. He is at position 1 among Top 10 chef in Italy. He is the partner of Osteria Francescana, a three-Michelin-star eatery situated in Modena, Italy which has been recorded in the position no. 5 at The World’s 50 Best Restaurant Awards since 2010 and got top appraisals from ESPRESSO, Gambero Rosso and the Touring Club guides.
